HCLK is AHB's clock, the main clock that governs the AHB bus. The HCLK is derived from the system clock through the AHB prescaler.

<img width="720" height="452" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9734444d-7c41-4fde-9bcc-bdcddba2a021" />

All AHB peripherals are clocked by the HCLK.

<img width="1137" height="562" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a0c99315-946d-433f-87b8-bb722c902173" />

PLL engine is used to boost clock higher than HSI and HSE.
